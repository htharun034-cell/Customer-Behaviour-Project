#  Customer Behaviour Analytics

##  Project Overview

Customer Behaviour Analytics is a data analytics project that analyzes customer shopping behavior using Excel, SQL, Python, and Power BI. The project aims to identify purchasing patterns, customer preferences, seasonal trends, and revenue drivers to help businesses make better marketing and sales decisions.

---

##  Problem Statement

Understanding customer behavior is essential for improving customer satisfaction and increasing business revenue.

This project answers important business questions such as:

- Which product categories generate the highest revenue?
- How do customer demographics influence purchasing behavior?
- Which season generates the highest sales?
- Which payment methods are most preferred?
- Does subscription status increase customer spending?
- Which shipping method is preferred by customers?
- How does purchase frequency affect customer value?

##  Key Insights

- Clothing and Accessories generate the highest purchase volume.
- Electronics products have the highest average purchase value.
- Subscribers spend more than non-subsscribers.
- Debit Card and PayPal are the most preferred payment methods.
- Express Shipping is the most frequently selected shipping option.
- Summer season generates the highest revenue.
- Male customers contribute a larger share of total revenue.
- Purchase frequency has a significant impact on customer value.

---

##  Business Recommendations

- Increase promotions during Summer to maximize revenue.
- Strengthen the subscription program to improve customer retention.
- Expand premium electronics offerings.
- Focus marketing campaigns on high-performing customer segments.
- Improve express shipping services.
- Offer personalized recommendations based on purchase history.
